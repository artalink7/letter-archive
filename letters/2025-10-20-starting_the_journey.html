ğŸ¬ Weekly Movie

For this week's movie pick, I have chosen Before Sunrise directed by Richard Linklater.

The main characters, Jesse and Celine, walking around Vienna.

Before Sunrise was released 30 years ago and gained immediate success. Among millennials, it marked an entire generation of romantic-drama movies.
The protagonists are Jesse and Celine. They meet on a train while traveling through Europe and, after talking for a while in the train seats, they decide to jump off together at the next stop: Vienna. There, they spend the whole day together and... okay, not gonna spoil you.

The movie is extremely well directed. Linklater alternates static and tracking shots without exceeding with the camera movement. Some shots of Vienna are so beautiful you could just screenshot, print, and hang them on your bedroom wall.

After watching it, I thought about how difficult it is to go on a first date with someone without feeling the need to fill those awkward silent moments. And even if you're good at "do the talking", can you really keep up for a whole day?
Well, that's what Jesse and Celine did...
