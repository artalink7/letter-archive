Joe Pesci is mostly famous for his gangster roles in hollywood movies. I think his most memorable role is GoodFellas' Tommy DeVito (I already talked about this movie in a previous letter).

It was a month ago I discover his works in music. With great surprise I found out Joe is also a talented singer!

Pesci... Still Singing is a record containing covers of famous standard-jazz tracks. A tribute to famous jazz songs from Joe Pesci and his band.

Joe has quite a particular voice, he gives the songs an interesting flavour. My favourite from the album is They Say That Falling In Love is Wonderful.
Originally, the song was composed for a musical aired in the early fifties. It slowly became a standard, with more and more artists re-intepreting the song.
I think most of the people of my age know this song from the movie Spiderman 3. Indeed, the song is sung by Kirsten Dunst in the opening act of the movie.
